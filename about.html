About

As a recent graduate of the University of Florida, I have learned the art of what it means to be a free, self-loving individual. This newfound independence ignited a spark in me for wanting to use my creative side to design materials, engage with the community and develop strategic plans for other individuals and businesses to benefit from.

I have spent the past year and will continue this year working on my masters in web design. I have gained much knowledge in HTML coding and CSS styling languages. I will continue to master these skills as well as learn more advanced web practices.

I am very confident in who I am as a person and am able to execute any process, plan or event with full organization and in a timely manner. I am comfortable reaching out to people, directing people and giving order to the team.
